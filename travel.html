Hotels near the Venue

Although there is no hotel block, there are plenty of beautiful hotels in the city of Houston. These are just a few of our recommendations.

Hotel Saint Augustine

Luxurious boutique hotel adjacent to The Menil Collection, near Rice University and Hermann Park, and a 10 minute drive from our venue!

The Chifley Houston, Tapestry Collection by Hilton

This modern Uptown Houston hotel is located next to The Galleria, a half mile from The Arboretum and Nature Center, and a 10 minute drive from our venue!

DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Houston, Greenway Plaza

This comfortable hotel boasts spacious rooms with panoramic views of Houston's skyline and is located near Rice Village, the River Oaks District, and a 10 minute drive from our venue!

La Colombe d'Or Hotel

Set in a 1920s mansion, this boutique hotel is located in the heart of Montrose, within walking distance of cafes, shops and galleries, and a 10 minute drive from our venue!
