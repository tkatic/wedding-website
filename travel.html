Travel & Stay

River Oaks Garden Club

The Journey

Getting Here

By Air — Bush Intercontinental (IAH)

Houston's main international airport is approximately 30–35 minutes from the venue. Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) and rental cars are readily available. We recommend booking early for the best rates.

By Air — Hobby Airport (HOU)

A smaller, convenient airport located about 20–25 minutes from River Oaks. A great option if flying Southwest Airlines or from nearby cities.

By Car

The venue is centrally located in Houston. Parking is available nearby. We also encourage rideshare throughout the evening so everyone can celebrate freely.

Accommodations

Where to Stay

Hotel Granduca Houston

An Italian-inspired boutique luxury hotel just minutes from the venue. Elegant rooms, beautiful grounds, and a wonderful restaurant — perfect for the weekend.

The Post Oak Hotel

Houston's most luxurious hotel, located in the Uptown/Galleria area. A short drive to the venue with world-class amenities and stunning city views.

Hotel ZaZa Houston Museum District

A stylish, boutique option in the Museum District — close to the venue and the heart of Houston's cultural scene.

Le Méridien Houston Downtown

A refined downtown option for those who want to explore the city before and after the wedding weekend.

While You're Here

Things to Do

Houston

The Montrose Neighborhood

Vibrant restaurants, art galleries, boutique shops, and green spaces. The heartbeat of Houston's creative scene — and our future neighborhood.

The Menil Collection

One of the great art museums of the world, free and open to all. Set amid beautiful oak-lined grounds just minutes from the venue.

Buffalo Bayou Park

Scenic trails, kayaking, and sweeping city skyline views along Houston's most beloved green corridor. A beautiful way to spend a morning in the city.
